About me

Designer who builds.
Developer who creates.

I'm Inês — a multimedia designer and informatics engineering student based in Coimbra, Portugal. I don't just make things look good; I understand how products work from the inside out.

That's rare in a creative. Most designers can't read a codebase. Most developers can't make something feel human. I live in both worlds — which means I can take your technical product and translate it into content that actually connects with real people.

I've built full-stack apps, run brand activations, designed podcasts, and created UGC for AI tools. All of that feeds into one thing: making tech startups look as capable as they are.
